Click to re-center brush

This customization is implemented by changing the type of the brushâ€™s overlay element from overlay to selection so that clicking and dragging on the overlay element behaves identically to clicking and dragging on the brush selection. (The overlay element is the invisible rect element that receives pointer events when clicking outside the brush selection.) When the overlay is clicked and before brushing starts, the brush is recentered around the pointer, such that the brush remains centered around the pointer during brushing.
